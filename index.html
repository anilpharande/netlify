Anil Pharande

Anil Pharande, founder of Pharande Spaces, is recognized as one of Pune & PCMC’s most respected real estate leaders and a visionary in modern urban development. With more than 30 years of experience in the real estate industry, he has played a significant role in transforming the Pimpri-Chinchwad region through premium residential communities, integrated townships, luxury villas, and commercial developments focused on quality living and future-ready infrastructure.

Starting his journey in 1994 with determination, vision, and a commitment toward quality construction, Anil Pharande identified the long-term growth potential of PCMC much before many major developers entered the market. At a time when the region was primarily known for affordable housing and industrial development, he believed that infrastructure growth, connectivity, and planned urban development would eventually transform PCMC into one of Pune’s leading real estate destinations.

Under his leadership, Pharande Spaces gradually expanded from small residential projects into large-scale township developments and modern communities across locations including Punawale, Ravet, Moshi, Spine Road, Talegaon, and Urse. Landmark projects such as Vaarivana, Puneville, Vaanya, Kairosa, and L-axis reflect the company’s commitment toward integrated township living, modern architecture, premium amenities, and long-term value creation.

Anil Pharande’s contribution extends beyond real estate development. As the former President of CREDAI Pune, he has actively participated in urban planning discussions, infrastructure initiatives, and industry development efforts focused on shaping a better and more connected Pune. His technical expertise, long-term vision, and customer-centric philosophy have helped Pharande Spaces establish itself as one of the trusted real estate brands in Pune & PCMC.

Known for his focus on infrastructure-led growth, Anil Pharande strongly believes that real estate should go beyond buildings and create better lifestyles, stronger communities, and sustainable urban environments. Several Pharande Spaces developments have delivered strong long-term appreciation and investment value, with many projects witnessing significant growth due to strategic location selection and future-focused planning.

Today, Anil Pharande continues to inspire the next generation of urban development through his leadership, innovation, and commitment toward quality construction. His journey reflects persistence, visionary thinking, and a dedication toward building future-ready communities that contribute positively to Pune & PCMC’s evolving real estate landscape.
