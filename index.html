About

Building Pune's
Future, One Project at a Time

Anil Pharande is the founder of Pharande Spaces and one of the most respected names in Pune's real estate industry. With over 30 years of experience, he has played a significant role in shaping the residential and commercial landscape of Pune & PCMC.

Known for his strong vision, technical expertise, and dedication to quality construction, Anil Pharande has helped Pharande Spaces establish itself as a trusted real estate brand since 1994. His contribution to urban development and modern infrastructure has made him a recognized figure across the industry.

Beyond building structures, his philosophy centers on creating communities — spaces that improve lifestyles, foster belonging, and contribute positively to the city's growth.

Quality Construction

Every development under his leadership upholds the highest standards of construction quality, ensuring long-term value for homeowners.

Integrated Townships

Pioneer in developing large-scale integrated communities combining premium amenities, green spaces, and excellent connectivity.

Customer Trust

Decades of timely delivery and transparent practices have built unwavering trust among thousands of home buyers across Pune & PCMC.

Industry Leadership

Served as President of CREDAI Pune, actively shaping city planning policies and elevating industry standards region-wide.

Journey

Three Decades of
Vision & Growth

1994 — The Beginning

Founding of Pharande Spaces

Starting with limited resources, strong goodwill, and financial support, Anil Pharande laid the foundation of Pharande Spaces with a long-term vision for growth and excellence in residential development.

Early Years

Small Residential Buildings

The company began with small residential projects, steadily earning customer trust through consistent quality, honest dealings, and attention to detail on every build.

Growth Phase

Expansion into Bungalows & Towers

Success led to expansion into bungalows, residential towers, and premium apartments across key locations including Punawale, Ravet, Moshi, Spine Road, Pradhikaran, and Urse.

Maturity Phase

Integrated Townships & Luxury Villas

Pharande Spaces evolved into developing large-scale townships, luxury villas, and integrated communities designed around quality living, modern infrastructure, and lifestyle convenience.

Today

Trusted Developer, Pune & PCMC

Today, the company stands among Pune's most trusted real estate developers — with millions of square feet delivered, a strong reputation for timely delivery, and communities built for the future.
